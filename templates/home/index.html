New Feature: Private Repositories

The development of TailR is ongoing. We are pleased to annnounce a new major feature: Private Repositories. Private repositories are not visible to the public and can only be accessed by its owner (API authentication is done by the generated tokens as for pushing new data). You can change the privacy settings of your repositories on the respective repository page.
